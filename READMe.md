" project scope: The goal of this project is to develop a Real Estate Market Intelligence Database. The application is designed to analyze housing prices and property features to provide actionable market insights. "

"Key Objectives:

Data Integration: Process and store comprehensive housing data, specifically focusing on the Bengaluru house price dataset.

Trend Forecasting: Implement price trend forecasting to help stakeholders understand market trajectories.

Location-Based Indexing: Develop an indexing system to categorize and retrieve property data based on specific geographic locations.

High-Level Goals: To provide a structured environment for analyzing property features and their direct impact on market value."

Users: This database is intended for individuals and entities within the application domain who require data-driven real estate insights:

Real Estate Analysts: Professionals looking for historical price trends and feature-based comparisons to advise clients or firms.

Property Investors: Users who need to evaluate the potential ROI of a location based on indexed market data and forecasts.

Data Scientists/Researchers: Individuals interested in housing market dynamics and the correlation between property attributes and final pricing.
